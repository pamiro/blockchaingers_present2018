Empowering future economy

Challenge: Technology

We have today autonomous robots, self-driving cars, AI, internet of things. Can we make the new machine economy serve the best interest of humanity by design, without:

  • central planning
  • regulatory oversight
  • monopolization
  • fraud

Challenge: Society

We facing new stage in evolution, when people do not make goods by own hand and provide services directly to each other, but instead, design machines and create it systems to serve each other.

  • In new machine economy in place of supply chains, we have supply graph covering whole globe.
  • Large corporates are challenged by small automated businesses operating on global scale.
  • The working class is disappearing, people are forced to compete for more creative and intellectual jobs.

Challenge: Fairness

We should make sure that new economy is built on:

  • Transparency
  • Fair trade
  • Collaboration

We should make sure that new economy foster invention and innovation, rewards only true and healthy entrepreneurship. There is no place for manipulative commerce based on unfair economic position, people focus on making machine that satisfy demands in the best possible.

Protocol

The protocol facilitates smart contract / risk taking / deal making between producers, suppliers, manufactures. Each of the participant in protocol publishes on the network own demands, supplies, costs, capabilities, attributes. Miners are the entrepreneurs, they employ machine learning and find optimal contact solution that fits multiple parties.
